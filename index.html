Resident Services

What can we help you with?

Access city services online, 24/7. No lines, no waiting.

Permits & Licenses
Apply for business licenses, building permits, and special event permits online. Track your application status in real-time.
Report an Issue
Report potholes, streetlight outages, graffiti, and other issues. We'll route your request to the right department automatically.
Pay Bills & Taxes
Pay property taxes, utility bills, and parking tickets online. Set up automatic payments and never miss a due date.
Parks & Recreation
Register for programs, reserve facilities, and get park permits. Explore trails, sports leagues, and community events.
